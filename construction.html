WTD CNC Industrial Complex

Under Construction
Total Area 22,000 m² 41 Industrial Units 16 Commercial Units

Main Contractor — Industrial Facility

Contour for Construction is currently executing WTD CNC Industrial Complex as the main contractor for Egypt’s first specialized complex dedicated to CNC industries. The project is designed to bring together suppliers and manufacturers across various CNC-related industries, mold manufacturers, and heat-treatment specialist within a single integrated complex, requiring specialized construction solutions tailored to the operational and technical demands of CNC manufacturing.

Spanning a total built-up area of 22,000 square meters, the complex comprises 41 industrial units, in addition to 16 commercial units carefully planned and constructed to serve both industrial and commercial activities.

Contour for Construction is responsible for the ongoing execution of construction works, ensuring:

  • Efficient infrastructure and utility systems
  • High-quality structural and finishing standards
  • Industrial layouts optimized for CNC operations
  • Safe, functional, and production-ready facilities

Upon completion, CNC Industrial Complex will demonstrate Contour for Construction’s capability to execute technically demanding industrial facilities, meeting the specialized structural, infrastructure, and operational requirements of CNC manufacturing.

WTD New Traffic Industrial Park

Under Construction
Total Area 60,000 m² 113 Factories 60 Commercial Units

Contour for Construction is executing The New Traffic Complex as the main contractor, comprising a fully integrated mix of industrial and commercial facilities.The project involves the construction of 113 factories and 60 commercial units with flexible layouts.covering a total built-up area of 52,000 m² for industrial facilities and 8,000 m² for commercial spaces.

The complex is engineered to accommodate a wide range of industrial and trade activities, requiring adaptable structural systems and robust infrastructure solutions.

Contour for Construction is responsible for the ongoing execution of construction works, ensuring:

  • High-capacity infrastructure and utility networks
  • Durable industrial-grade structural systems
  • Flexible layouts supporting diverse manufacturing uses
  • Integrated logistical and service infrastructure ensuring operational continuity

The project is designed to support manufacturers and small to medium-sized workshops through a fully equipped, production-ready environment, placing strong emphasis on functionality, efficiency, and long-term operational performance.

Upon completion, the project will mark Contour for Construction’s second delivery of a large-scale, multi-use industrial development, reflecting our expertise in executing complex industrial construction aligned with demanding operational and logistical requirements.
