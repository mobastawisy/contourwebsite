Trevi Mall

TREVI Mall sets a new standard for retail, dining and lifestyle destinations in Al-Shorouk City. Strategically positioned within a growing upscale residential catchment, walking distance from the British University in Egypt and 10 minutes from Madinaty Gate, the mall is designed to serve daily needs while offering a modern, convenient, and high-quality shopping experience for residents, visitors, and business owners alike.

The project brings together a carefully curated mix of retail outlets, cafés, restaurants, and essential services, complemented by well-planned circulation, comfortable open areas, and efficient parking solutions — creating a balanced environment that combines functionality with contemporary design.

Prime Location

Trevi Mall enjoys a strategic location in the heart of Al-Shorouk City, positioned to serve a rapidly growing residential and educational community. The project benefits from strong visibility, accessibility, and long-term commercial demand.

10 minutes from Madinaty Gate
Walking distance from BUE
20 minutes from Rehab City
Between El-Shorouk City Rd & El-Sadat Rd

Set within a thriving residential environment, Trevi Mall is surrounded by prestigious communities including La Vista–Casa, Granda Al-Shorouk, VIVENS Compound and a lot more upscale residential compounds and villas. Its proximity to major traffic corridors ensures seamless accessibility, positioning the mall as a natural lifestyle and retail destination with strong daily footfall.
