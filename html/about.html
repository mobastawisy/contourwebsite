Our Evolution

Originally established as a construction and engineering firm, Contour has successfully delivered complex industrial and commercial projects nationwide. Today, we expand this expertise into real estate development, launching our first commercial development project, TREVI Mall in El Shorouk City.

This evolution allows us to control the full lifecycle of a project—from land vision and feasibility to design, construction, and final operation.
